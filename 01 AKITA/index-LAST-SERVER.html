First time?

This is a step by step instruction on how to buy AKITA INU with Metamask.

01 Download Metamask

Got to Metamask and load the app. Follow the in-app-tutorial, log in and get started. Don't forget to write down your private pass-phrase (Don't lose it!).

02 Buy Ethereum

Get some ETH at your prefered exchange.

03 transfer to Metamask

Copy your Metamask adress and transfer ETH to your wallet.

04 Connect to uniswap

Find the official AKITA contract adress here (copy contract on top right corner) and connect your wallet to uniswap.

05 Swap

Select From: ETH; To: Paste in official AKITA contract adress and click import. Put in the ETH-amount u wish to swap. Make sure u got enough ETH for the transaction fee (edit the fee to make sure transaction runs smoothly).

06 add liquidity

If u wish, add liquidity to the ETH-AKITA pair on uniswap.
