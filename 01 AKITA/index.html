First time?

This is a step by step instruction on how to buy AKITA INU with Metamask.

01 - Download Metamask

Go to Metamask and load the app. Follow the in-app-tutorial, log in and get started. Don't forget to write down your private pass-phrase (don't lose it!).

02 - Buy Ethereum

Get some ETH at your preferred exchange.

03 - Transfer to Metamask

Copy your Metamask adress and transfer ETH to your wallet.

04 - Connect to Uniswap

Find the official AKITA contract adress here (copy contract on top right corner) and connect your wallet to Uniswap.

05 - Swap

Select From: ETH; To: Paste in official AKITA contract adress and click import. Put in the ETH-amount you wish to swap. Make sure you got enough ETH for the transaction fee (edit the fee to make sure transaction runs smoothly).

06 - Add liquidity

If you wish, add liquidity to the ETH-AKITA pair on Uniswap.
